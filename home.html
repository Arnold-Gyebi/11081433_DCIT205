PROGRAMMES OFFERED
Degree Programs
At University of Ghana, we offer a range of comprehensive degree programs in computer science to cater to diverse interests and career goals of our students. Our programs are designed to provide a strong foundation in theoretical and practical aspects of computer science, equipping graduates with the skills necessary to excel in the-evolving tech industry.
Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science
Our undergraduate program in computer science is to provide students with a strong understanding of fundamental computer science principles and techniques. Through a well-rounded curriculum, students gain proficiency in programming languages, algorithms, data structures, software development, and more. Our program also emphasizes hands-on learning through project-based coursework and internships, allowing students to their knowledge to real-world problems.
Master's Degree in Computer Science
Our master's program in computer science is designed for students who wish to deepen their understanding of computer science and specialize in specific areas of interest. We offer both a thesis and a non-thesis option, giving students the flexibility choose a pathway that aligns with their career goals. In addition to core coursework, our master's program offers a wide range of elective courses covering advanced computer science topics such as artificial intelligence, science, cybersecurity, and more.
PhD Programmes
Our doctoral programme is a full-time research programme with expected duration of 4-years. For the successful completion of this programme, a high level of commitment is required. Students are therefore expected to reside close to the University and be a full-time participant in the program. With guidance from postgraduate committee, Ph.D. students will create a personalized program of study that includes a major area of specialization and at least two minor areas of specialization.
