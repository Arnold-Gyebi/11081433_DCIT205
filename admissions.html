ADMISSIONS

UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAM

We offer rigorous undergraduate programs in Computer Science designed to equip students with a solid foundation in computing, programming, algorithms, and problem-solving skills. Prospective students interested in pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science are encouraged to apply through the university's admission portal. Admission criteria typically include meeting the minimum academic requirements set by the university, including relevant high school qualifications and performance in required entrance examinations. For further information or to explore additional details, kindly visit the undergraduate sectioon of the University of Ghana Admissions portal.

GRADUATE PROGRAMS

Our graduate programs cater to those seeking advanced studies in Computer Science. We offer Master's and Ph.D. programs focusing on specialized areas within the field, providing opportunities for research, innovation, and academic excellence. Prospective graduate students are required to meet specific academic qualifications, which may include possessing a relevant bachelor's degree, satisfactory performance in standardized tests, and fulfilling other department-specific prerequisites. For further information or to explore additional details, kindly visit the graduate sectioon of the University of Ghana Admissions portal.

APPLICATION PROCESS

Applicants for both undergraduate and graduate programs should submit their applications through the University of Ghana's official admissions portal during the designated application period. Detailed instructions and admission requirements specific to the Computer Science Department can be found on the department's website or the university's admissions office. Please ensure to review and follow the outlined guidelines and deadlines for a successful application.

SCHOLARSHIPS AND FINANCIAL AID

The Department of Computer Science, in collaboration with the university, offers various scholarship opportunities and financial aid packages for eligible students. These scholarships may be merit-based, need-based, or tied to specific achievements. Prospective students are encouraged to explore the available scholarship options and criteria through the university's financial aid office or the department's website.

CONTACT INFORMATION

For any inquiries regarding admissions, specific program requirements, or application procedures, please feel free to reach out to our Admissions Office on the first floor of International House, #1Annie Jiagge Road, University Campus, Legon or visit the University of Ghana's official website for further information.

We look forward to welcoming passionate and talented individuals to join our dynamic academic community at the University of Ghana's Department of Computer Science!
